There are 8 Bakhroshungz dialects: Central, Northern, Northwestern, Hüdpaqhtheth (meaning: Border-part), Western, Southern, Southeastern, and Eastern.
The rest of the pages, as disclaimed in the starting page, describe the Central dialect. This page describes the other dialects in comparison to the Central dialect.
